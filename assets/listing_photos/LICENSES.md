# Listing Photo Demo Assets

These photos are illustrative demo assets for FlatFeed synthetic listings. They
show Berlin multi-family residential buildings, but they do not depict the
specific synthetic listing address shown in the bot.

All files are local resized copies of Wikimedia Commons images.
